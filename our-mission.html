About Energy Terminal

Our Mission

We exist to bridge the gap between students and the energy industry — making this exciting sector accessible, navigable, and exciting for the next generation of leaders.

Why Energy Terminal Exists

The energy sector is one of the most consequential industries of our time — shaping climate outcomes, economic opportunity, and global development. Yet for most students, it remains opaque and hard to enter.

Energy Terminal was founded to change that. We provide the resources, community, and connections students need to discover, explore, and launch careers in energy and climate.

From renewable energy to oil & gas, from policy to finance — we cover the full spectrum of the energy world, without gatekeeping or jargon.

"Our goal is simple: every student who wants to work in energy should have the tools, knowledge, and network to make it happen."

— Energy Terminal Founders

The Energy Terminal Podcast

In-depth conversations with energy professionals, founders, policymakers, and investors — giving students an inside look at careers and ideas shaping our energy future.

About the Podcast

Each episode features a candid conversation with an energy professional from a wide range of backgrounds — renewable energy developers, ESG investors, policy advocates, climate tech founders, and more.

Our goal is to make this exciting industry feel accessible, not intimidating. No jargon, no gatekeeping — just honest conversations about what it is like to work in energy.

Resource Hub

Energy Verticals

The energy industry is not monolithic. Explore the specific sub-sectors that power our world — and find where you want to build your career.

☀️

Renewable

Solar Energy

Utility-scale farms, rooftop installations, and distributed generation. One of the fastest-growing sectors with vast career opportunities globally.

💨

Renewable

Wind Energy

Onshore and offshore wind are central to the clean energy transition. Engineering, project development, and supply chain roles in high demand.

⚛️

Low-Carbon

Nuclear Energy

Advanced reactor designs and SMRs have sparked a resurgence. Nuclear offers reliable, low-carbon baseload power and exciting technical careers.

🔋

Storage

Energy Storage

Batteries and emerging storage technologies are critical for grid reliability. An exploding sector with deep technical and commercial tracks.

🟢

Fuels

Green Hydrogen

A key decarbonization tool for hard-to-abate sectors like steel, shipping, and aviation. Still early stage with significant investment flowing in.

🛢️

Traditional

Oil & Gas

The world's largest energy source is transforming. Understand the energy transition from within one of the most impactful industries on Earth.

🏙️

Infrastructure

Grid & Utilities

The electric grid is being rebuilt for the 21st century. Careers in grid modernization, demand response, and smart infrastructure abound.

📊

Markets

Carbon Markets

Voluntary and compliance carbon markets are growing fast. A complex financial ecosystem with roles spanning trading, project development, and policy.

🌊

Emerging

Marine & Tidal

Ocean energy from tidal streams, waves, and OTEC is an emerging frontier with long-term potential for coastal and island communities worldwide.

🚗

Transport

EV & Mobility

The electrification of transport is reshaping the auto industry, grid demand, and urban infrastructure. From charging networks to battery tech.

🏗️

Efficiency

Building Efficiency

Buildings consume 40% of global energy. Retrofits, smart systems, and green building design offer impactful and growing career pathways.

🌿

Agriculture

Agri-Energy

From biogas and biomass to agrivoltaics and rural electrification — the intersection of food systems and energy is a rich area of innovation.
